Permanently delete capture {{ "%03d"|format(capture.sequence_number) }}?

This conflicts with the append-only record, on purpose. Morningstar normally never destroys evidence — but your right to remove sensitive content outranks the ledger. Two options:

  • Hide keeps the evidence intact and just removes it from normal view. Fully reversible. Prefer this if you can.
  • Permanent deletion irreversibly blanks this capture's text, its annotations, and the ledger entries that carried its content. The record of when a capture existed, its ordering, and the hash chain remain, and the deletion itself is recorded as an event — but the content cannot be recovered and can no longer be verified against its hash.

Anything you exported earlier still contains the content; deletion here does not reach backups or exports.
