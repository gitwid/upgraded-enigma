Breath

Mindfulness begins with the breath. Smooth natural breathing is, almost always, the answer. Whether it be stress, anxiety, conflict or just feeling low, focussing on the breath and finding the right rhythm is one of the simplest ways of creating a space for your own self, that's safe, relaxed and leads to renewal and calm.
